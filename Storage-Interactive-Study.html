18 ESFR — Wet Pipe Only, 36" Clearance 🚒
ESFR = wet pipe ONLY — no exceptions. CMSA and ESFR both require 36-inch clearance (top of storage to deflector).
ESFR Requirements
🚫 DRY PIPE + ESFR = NOT ALLOWED
No exceptions in NFPA 13
✅ WET PIPE + ESFR = Required
ESFR is always wet pipe system

🧠 ESFR = Early Suppression Fast Response. Huge drops, high pressure. Requires wet pipe for immediate water delivery — delay from dry pipe air purge would be catastrophic.
