# NICET / NFPA Study Integration

Goal: 15+ focused minutes per workday, visual and scenario-based. Study slots
fit inside the shift plan at **+3h (reset+admin)** or **+5h (post-lunch
refocus)** — whichever block has slack that day — or off-shift on request.

## Weekly rotation (default — adjust to exam outline when Maurice shares it)
- **Mon** New material: one chapter section, taught visually (diagram/table first).
- **Tue** Drill: pop quiz on Monday's topic. Log score in daily log.
- **Wed** Scenario day: one worked real-world design/ITM scenario.
- **Thu** Weak-spot review: re-drill whatever scored lowest this week.
- **Fri** Mixed quiz across the week + 3-line self-summary.

## Coaching rules for study
- Always scenario-framed: "You walk into a warehouse with 25-ft double-row
  racks…" beats "What does 25.3.1 say?"
- Wrong answers are the curriculum: track misses in the daily log and resurface
  them 2 days later (spaced repetition).
- When a topic keeps missing, offer to BUILD a new visual quiz app for it —
  building is how Maurice learns best.
