Rule 2 — Ceiling Pocket Sprinklers: The 6-Condition Exception (§10.2.9)
§10.2.9.1 — Sprinklers shall be required in ALL ceiling pockets. (Default rule — always starts here.)

The exception at §10.2.9.2 allows pocket sprinklers to be omitted only when ALL SIX conditions below are satisfied simultaneously. If even one fails → pocket sprinklers required.

  • 1
    Volume of the unprotected pocket ≤ 1,000 ft³ (28 m³) §10.2.9.2(1) — Calculate: L × W × depth of raised area in feet
  • 2
    Depth of pocket ≤ 36 in. (900 mm) §10.2.9.2(2) — Maurice = 31.5 in. ✓ PASSES
  • 3
    Entire floor area beneath the pocket is protected at the lower ceiling elevation §10.2.9.2(3) — Every sq. ft. of floor under the raised section must be covered by the 7′-2″ sprinklers
  • 4
    Combined volume of all unprotected pockets within 10 ft of each other ≤ 1,000 ft³ §10.2.9.2(4) — Add volumes of all nearby pockets; total must stay under limit
  • 5
    Interior finish of the pocket is noncombustible or limited-combustible §10.2.9.2(5) — Drywall, concrete, metal deck OK · Wood paneling or combustible tile = FAIL
  • 6
    Quick-response (QR) sprinklers are used throughout the entire compartment §10.2.9.2(6) — ALL sprinklers in the room must be QR-rated, not just those near the pocket
EXAM TRAP — Condition 6: A single standard-response sprinkler anywhere in the room kills the exception. The QR requirement applies to every sprinkler in the compartment, not just the ones adjacent to the pocket.

Cloud & Reference Rules
Cloud Ceiling Cross-Section — Two-Level Sprinkler Layout
Cloud ceiling cross-section. Clouds wider than 4 ft require sprinklers at the structural ceiling (in gaps between clouds) AND below each cloud. Under-cloud deflector must be within 12 in. of cloud bottom (§10.2.7.3.4).
Key Cloud Rules
§10.2.7.3.2 — Sprinklers shall be installed under fixed obstructions over 4 ft (1.2 m) wide.
§10.2.7.3.4 — Under-cloud deflector position:
No more than 12 in. (300 mm) below the bottom of the obstruction.
Your plan decoded:
○ Open circle = ceiling-level sprinkler in gap between clouds (at structural ceiling)
● Filled circle = under-cloud sprinkler below cloud panel
Decision flow for any obstruction:
  1. Is the obstruction fixed (not moveable)?
  2. Measure the width. > 4 ft? → Under-obstruction sprinkler required.
  3. Position deflector ≤ 12 in. below obstruction bottom.
  4. Position ceiling sprinkler per A/B table at right.
